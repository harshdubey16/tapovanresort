1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or make a booking enquiry through Tapovan Resort's website, we may collect the following information:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected solely to:

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is used only for the purposes stated above.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies to improve your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information.
